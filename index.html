How US Senators Related to Each Other on Twitter
With the information of Twitter following/followed, a network of 45 US senators can be conducted to show the relationships between them.

After comparing the frequency of their names being mentioned by New York Times (NYT) in the past 20 years, it is clear that NFL has always got more hits in NYT articles than NBA, which means the American coverages also focus more on NFL.
Edges
After removing other followers who are not in the 45 US senators’ list, the number of edges in the network is 990, which means there are 990 Twitter-following or followed relationships lying in these 45 senators.
In-degree
The highest in-degree node in the network is Republican senator SenatorEnzi. The highest value means the senator is most followed by people among the 45 senators, which also implies that his tweets are more likely to be seen and have the widest influence among these senators.
Out-degree
The highest out-degree node in the network is Republican senator LisaMurkowski. The value means the senator follows a maximum number of senators among the 45, which also implies that he/she can get in touch with the largest amount of tweets from these people, in other words, he/she can receive most information from tweets of them.
Betweenness
The highest betweenness in the network is also Republican senator LisaMurkowski with the value of 109.10. The value means there are most paths going through him, which also implies that the senator stands at the central position of the network. It seems that he plays the most favorable role to control the network.
Reciprocity
By calculating the reciprocity of the network, we can find the value (> reciprocity(net)[1] 0.5182186) is just around the 50% of the possibility.
A researching result shown on Quora according to a paper from KAIST tells that "Twitter reciprocity is 22.1%".The figure of the reciprocity (0.518...) of this senators’ network is not high within the 0-1 range, but compared by general Twitter reciprocity (around 22%) provided before, the figure itself is much higher than the general situation. This shows that the network of 45 US senators is more interactive than general one. These senators from different parties stay highly interactive connections among them.
